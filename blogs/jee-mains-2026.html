JEE Main 2026: Do’s & Don’ts for Exam Day

NTA's guidelines for JEE Main 2026 exam day emphasize arriving early, carrying essentials like admit card and ID, and following hall protocols to avoid disqualification in this computer-based test held in January and April sessions. These dos and don'ts ensure smooth conduct for over 1.2 million aspirants, promoting focus on 90 questions across Physics, Chemistry, and Math in 180 minutes. Adhering strictly maximizes performance and percentile scores for JEE Advanced eligibility.

What are the Do’s & Don’ts for JEE Main 2026 Exam Day and Their Benefits

Do’s include reaching the center 1 hour early for frisking and biometrics, while don'ts prohibit electronic gadgets to maintain integrity; these rules, updated for 2026, prevent disruptions and ensure fair evaluation. Benefits range from reduced stress to accurate scoring in the multi-shift format.

  • Do carry printed admit card, photo ID (Aadhaar/PAN), and passport-sized photo; this verifies identity swiftly, avoiding entry delays and biometric mismatches at the gate.
  • Do arrive by 8:30 AM for morning shift (9:00-12:00) or 1:30 PM for afternoon; early arrival allows buffer for traffic, easing anxiety and familiarizing with the secure center setup.
  • Don't bring prohibited items like mobiles, calculators, or notes; this upholds NTA's zero-tolerance policy, preventing cancellation and fostering a cheat-free environment.
  • Do follow dress code (light clothing, no metallic items) and cooperate with frisking; it speeds security checks, ensuring all candidates start on time without hindrances.
  • Don't discuss questions or leave early without permission; maintaining silence post-exam preserves answer key integrity, benefiting normalized scoring across shifts.

Effective Tips for Following Do’s & Don’ts on Exam Day

Prepare documents night before: Download admit card from jeemain.nta.ac.in 2-3 days prior, affix photo, and pack ID in a transparent pouch; this do ensures quick entry, avoiding last-minute rushes on January/April 2026 dates. Rehearse the routine to align with 2026 guidelines.

  • Do eat a light breakfast and stay hydrated but avoid excess; this sustains energy for the 3-hour CBT without bio-breaks, following don'ts on food in hall.
  • Plan travel to center (assigned via city preference); arrive 90 minutes early, wear comfortable shoes, and carry only permitted items like pen/pencil for rough work.
  • Do read instructions on screen carefully before starting; use on-screen calculator wisely, adhering to don'ts on external aids for accurate numerical answers.
  • Follow invigilator cues during biometrics and seating; don't touch keyboard until green signal, ensuring your session logs correctly for percentile calculation.
  • Post-exam, don't share OMR details immediately; review performance mentally and exit calmly to avoid stress while awaiting official keys.

Simulate exam day with mocks on NTA app; contact helpline (011-40759000) for queries on 2026 specifics like PwD accommodations.

Common Mistakes to Avoid on JEE Main 2026 Exam Day

Forget essentials: Arriving without admit card or ID leads to denied entry; always double-check the night before to comply with mandatory verification dos. Ignoring time leads to rushed travel in multi-city setups.

  • Don't carry banned gadgets even powered off; accidental detection causes disqualification—leave them home to follow strict don'ts and focus on test.
  • Avoid late arrival past 9:30 AM (morning) or 2:30 PM; gates close, forfeiting the shift—plan routes early per do’s for buffer time.
  • Don't wear restricted clothing like watches or heavy jewelry; metal detectors flag them, delaying frisking—opt for simple attire as per guidelines.
  • Refrain from arguing with staff or peeking at screens; this violates conduct don'ts, risking warnings or ejection—cooperate fully for smooth proceedings.
  • Don't submit prematurely without review; rushing ignores time management dos, potentially missing easy questions in the 75-attempt format.
  • Avoid post-exam discussions on portal; breaching don'ts can lead to scrutiny—wait for official analysis to maintain your score integrity.

Mastering do’s and don’ts for JEE Main 2026 exam day ensures focused performance and avoids pitfalls in this gateway to IITs and NITs. Prepare diligently, stay calm, and turn guidelines into your advantage for top percentiles.
